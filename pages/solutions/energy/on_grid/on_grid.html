Sustainable Energy for Smarter Operations with On-Grid Solutions

At Spinel Distribution, we specialize in delivering reliable on-grid solar solutions tailored to meet the energy demands of modern businesses. Our expert team understands the components required to successfully implement efficient solar power systems—from high-performance panels and inverters to seamless grid integration and energy optimization.

Businesses today require consistent and cost-effective power to maintain smooth operations and stay competitive. In an increasingly energy-conscious world, adopting on-grid solar solutions enables organizations to reduce electricity costs, improve energy efficiency, and support sustainability goals. With smart solar integration, businesses can enjoy stable power supply, minimize downtime, and drive long-term growth while contributing to a greener future.

10 - 30KW Complete On-Grid System

The 10KW / 20KW / 30KW complete on-grid solar system for household applications is an integrated photovoltaic solution designed to help residential users reduce electricity costs and maximize solar energy utilization. The system combines high-efficiency solar panels, a reliable grid-tied inverter, and optimized mounting and electrical components to ensure stable and long-term power generation. Operating in on-grid mode, the system converts solar energy into usable electricity for household consumption while exporting excess power to the utility grid. It is ideal for villas, residential buildings, and large households seeking clean energy solutions with fast return on investment and minimal maintenance requirements.

50KW / 60KW Grid-Tied Solution

The 50KW / 60KW grid-tied solar power solution is a complete on-grid photovoltaic system designed for commercial and small industrial applications. By integrating high-efficiency solar panels, commercial-grade grid-tied inverters, and optimized electrical and mounting components, the system delivers stable and efficient solar power generation with minimal operational complexity. This solution operates in grid-tied mode, supplying solar electricity directly to on-site loads while exporting surplus energy to the utility grid. It is ideal for factories, warehouses, office buildings, and commercial rooftops seeking cost-effective clean energy solutions and predictable long-term returns.

125/250kW Industrial System

The 125/250kW on-grid commercial and industrial solar power system is an integrated photovoltaic solution engineered for medium- to large-scale C&I applications. By combining high-efficiency solar modules, commercial-grade grid-tied inverters, and optimized mounting and electrical components, the system ensures stable and long-term solar energy generation under demanding operating conditions. Operating in grid-tied mode, the system supplies clean solar electricity directly to commercial or industrial loads while exporting excess power to the utility grid. It is ideally suited for factories, industrial parks, warehouses, and large commercial rooftops seeking to reduce electricity costs and improve sustainability performance.
