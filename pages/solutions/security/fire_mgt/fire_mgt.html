AVENAR – the entire family of our fire detection solutions

AVENAR detector 4000

A flexible series of nine detectors available in both manual and automatic addressing systems. Available as single, double and multi-sensor versions for customized applications. Equipped with Intelligent Signal Processing based on over 4,000 fire patterns, making it highly reliable with top detection performance. eSmog provides EMC robustness against invisible disturbance, such as cell phones and WiFi. Dual-Ray LED technology helps visibly distinguish between disturbance values – for instance, blue light for steam and red for smoke. The latest member of the AVENAR detector 4000 series is FAP-425 DOTCO-R. It fulfills the optical and thermal criteria, and it also detects and alarms carbon monoxide concentrations, complying already to the upcoming EN 54 norms on CO sensors.

Detector 500/520 series

This can be installed as a scattered-light smoke detector or multi-sensor detector with either conventional or digital technology. Additional gas detector can be applied as needed for applications, for instance, where a co detector is mandatory. Condition Monitoring enables extensive data analysis. Choice of 16 RAL colors to complement interiors.
Learn More

Aspirating smoke detectors

Complete range to cater for diverse special applications, including for conventional technology. Highly reliable: two integrated isolators maintain LSNi loop functions in case of wire interruption or short circuits. Noise-reduced and silent versions available. Possible obstruction or breakage can be detected with airflow monitoring.
Learn More

Spinel Wireless detectors

Future-proof and flexible – next-generation wireless fire detection with high connection reliability, thanks to a dual-band system with a multichannel. This – and the longer radio range – not only optimize project installation costs, but also reduce potential maintenance costs and effort.
Learn More

Linear beam detectors

Large halls, like conference centers, warehouses, shopping malls and museums can all be kept safe with this ideal solution spacious rooms with high ceilings. There’s a high tolerance for on-site or local vibration and structural movements thanks to building movement tracking, making fire detection highly reliable. Installation is made easy with a direct connection to the LSNi, and electronic support for detector alignment and automatic detector calibration. False alarms are reduced with Light Cancellation Technology which compensates for strong sunlight and artificial lighting.
Learn More

Linear heat detectors

Constricted and confined spaces are all protected from risk of fire with this flexible application. Linear heat detectors by Bosch feature an IP 65 rated enclosure with a higher resistance to extreme environmental conditions. Maintenance costs are minimized by continual self-monitoring of the sensor cable. The configurable system for fixed heat alarm detection and an intuitive LCD display makes commissioning and operation easy.
Learn More

Flame detectors

ATEX and IECEx-certified for potentially explosive atmospheres in zones 1, 21, 2 and 22. These devices detect fires which are not visible to the naked eye, such as smokeless liquid and gas fires, plus fires of materials and substances which contain carbon with strong smoke emissions. They operate reliably, even if the lens is obscured by oil, dust, water vapor or ice. False alarms are low thanks to different IR wavelengths and a combination of filters and signal processing techniques which have been configured to recognize factors such as flickering sunlight.
Learn More

Spinel Distribution AVIOTEC

All-in-one security and fire detection solution – 24/7. AVIOTEC can be fitted with existing surveillance cameras for practical installation. It provides fire protection to the same highly reliable standard both indoors and outdoors, during the day in all weather conditions and even overnight, thanks to IR. Flames and smoke are detected within seconds and deep-learning and AI-based algorithms can easily distinguish between harmless factors and real fire risks, making it extremely robust against false alarms. AVIOTEC has already been installed as an all-round solution in a wide array of large-scale industrial applications, from factories to warehouses and logistics.
