Counter-drone tracking cameras

Our cameras enhance your ability to detect and spot drones and other unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), which are a growing threat to public safety and critical infrastructure.

Drone detection and tracking capabilities

Advanced drone detection and tracking capabilities employ multi-sensor cameras that use long-range daylight and thermal optics to identify threats in real time, day or night.

Thermal detection

Cooled or uncooled thermal imaging can detect small UAVs at distances exceeding 5 km/​3miles.

High-zoom visible and thermal optics

Observe drones 24/7 using SD or HD optics, with zoom capabilities up to 60x.

Real-time visual ID

Live video and analytics instantly verify aerial objects, enabling rapid response.
