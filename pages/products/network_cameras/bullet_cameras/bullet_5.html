AXIS P1475-LE Bullet Camera

2 MP all-around AI-powered surveillance

Excellent 2 MP video in any light
Reduced storage cost with AV1 codec
Next-generation AI-powered analytics
Robust and impact-resistant
Built-in cybersecurity with Axis Edge Vault
This robust, cost-effective, AI-powered bullet camera delivers excellent 2 MP image quality in any light conditions. Built on ARTPEC-9, it supports AV1 codec and offers accelerated performance to run impressive analytics applications on the edge.
Excellent image quality in any light
AXIS P1475-LE offers excellent image quality and forensic details in 2 MP at 60 fps, even in harsh weather and environments. It features OptimizedIR for surveillance in pitch darkness up to 50 m (164 ft). And Lightfinder 2.0 and Forensic WDR ensure true colors and great detail in near darkness or challenging light. Axis Zipstream with support for AV1, H.264, and H.265 significantly lowers bandwidth and storage requirements and cost without compromising image quality. Furthermore, it features a 3-9 mm lens with a 115°-35° horizontal field of view for wide-area surveillance.
Robust with strong security
This impact-resistant, IK10-, IP66-, 67- and NEMA 4X-rated outdoor-ready camera can handle extreme temperatures from -40°C to 60 °C (-40 to 140°F) and withstand winds up to 50 m/s (112 mph) and is powered with PoE IEEE 802.3af/802.3at Type 1 Class 3. Axis Edge Vault, our hardware-based cybersecurity platform, safeguards the device and offers FIPS 140-3 Level 3 certified secure key storage and operation. In addition, electronic image stabilization provides smooth video in situations where a camera is subject to vibrations. What’s more, this robust camera is easy to install, maintain, and operate. For instance, it includes auto-rotation and remote zoom and focus.
Powerful analytics
Featuring a deep learning processing unit (DLPU), this AI-based camera runs advanced features and powerful analytics on the edge. For instance, it comes with AXIS Object Analytics preinstalled to detect, classify, track, and count humans, vehicles, and types of vehicles. With ARTPEC-9, this application offers enhanced performance at greater distances. AXIS Image Health Analytics will notify you if the image is blocked, degraded, under-exposed, or redirected. And AXIS Live Privacy Shield addresses privacy laws and regulations and offers AI-based dynamic masking so you can detect movement and activities while safeguarding privacy.
